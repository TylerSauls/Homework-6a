Time Passing

A story of the seasons

Winter

Snow on the ground, rain falling from the sky and a cold breeze in the air. A time full of people staying indoors, avoiding the icy weather, drinking hot-chocolate and waiting for the time to pass.

Spring

Flowers bloom and animals come out of hibernation. For most it is a fresh start, a time full of new beginings and oppotunites.

Summer

Sunshine, beaches, ice-cream and spending all day outdoors. The brighest and happiest time of the year, the day are long and filled with adventure.

Fall

Leaves covering the ground and the air is crisp and chilly. The weather is cooling down again and the days begin to shorten. The cycle of time then continues to repeat itself.
